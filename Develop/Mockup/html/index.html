Is it ok for dogs to sleep on the bed? Gemma Johnstone of The Spruce Pets: “There is nothing wrong with choosing to have your dog sleep elsewhere. Just be consistent, make sure they have a comfortable space and reward them for going there. Separate sleeping areas can be especially beneficial under certain circumstances.” (Reference)
